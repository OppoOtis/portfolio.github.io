Environment Design

What's the project

This was a VR project inspired by liminal spaces. I made this with a group of 3 in 5 days. The experience itself is multiple weird environments you walk through. Playing a bit with fears and feelings of the player. It is supposed to represent a dream or nightmare.

The process

I mainly worked on level design this project. Exploring liminal spaces and what feelings they could bring to someone walking through it. Designing the walkable layout and a system so you could walk in the same area but be in different rooms was a bit of a challenge. But really satisfying when it worked. We made it so you could walk inside a 6 x 8 meter space and not bump into anything. This made the environments really physical and more real.
