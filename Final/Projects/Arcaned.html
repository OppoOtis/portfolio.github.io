What's the project

Arcaned was a gamejam game, created with a small group of 4. The theme of the gamejam was "Lost and Found". We made a game where you had to rescue a lost arcade, by playing the games again and bring the arcade back to life. So we made small arcade games inside a game in 2 days time. Really fun project.

The process

I worked on the arcade and arcadefactory environments, made art for some of the arcade games and worked on a bit of code. I learned a lot about 3D modeling and level design, but also a bit of time management since it was a gamejam.
